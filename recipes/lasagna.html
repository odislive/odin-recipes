Ingredients

  • For the Meat Sauce:
    • 500 grams of ground beef
    • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 400 grams of canned crushed tomatoes
    • 200 grams of tomato sauce
    • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 30 milliliters of olive oil
  • For the Béchamel Sauce:
    • 50 grams of butter
    • 50 grams of all-purpose flour
    • 500 milliliters of milk
    • A pinch of ground nutmeg
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Lasagna:
    • 250 grams of lasagna noodles (approximately 10-12 sheets)
    • 200 grams of grated mozzarella cheese
    • 100 grams of grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Start by making the Meat Sauce:
    1. In a large saucepan, heat 30 milliliters of olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until they become translucent.
    3. Add the ground beef and cook until it's browned and crumbled, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
    4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.
    5. Season the sauce with dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  2. While the meat sauce is simmering, prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
    1. In a separate saucepan, melt 50 grams of butter over low heat.
    2. Add 50 grams of all-purpose flour and whisk continuously to make a roux. Cook for a few minutes until it's light golden in color.
    3. Gradually pour in 500 milliliters of milk while whisking to prevent lumps. Continue cooking and whisking until the sauce thickens, usually for about 5-7 minutes.
    4. Season the Béchamel sauce with a pinch of ground nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Remove it from the heat.
  3. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Now, it's time to assemble the lasagna:
    1. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    2. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    3. Spread a portion of the Béchamel sauce over the noodles.
    4. Sprinkle with a portion of grated mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes before serving.
